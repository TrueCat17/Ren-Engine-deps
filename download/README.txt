Warning!

These scripts are for maintenance purposes only.
Do not use them for general building.

-----

./progress.py:
	set progress states for all libs (need to download or copy).
./download.py:
	downloads libs from <links.txt> to <cache> directory.
./copy_and_clean.py:
	copies libs from <cache> to <../sources> with ignoring objects from <ignore/*> files.
